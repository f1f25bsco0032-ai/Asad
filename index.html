How I Work

Design Process

A structured, human-centered approach that keeps users at the center of every decision.

01

Discover

Understanding the user, the business, and the ecosystem through research.

  • User interviews & surveys
  • Competitive analysis
  • Heuristic evaluation
02

Define

Synthesizing research into clear problem statements and opportunity spaces.

  • Personas & JTBD
  • Affinity mapping
  • Design brief
03

Ideate

Generating and prioritizing ideas collaboratively — quantity before quality.

  • Crazy 8s, sketches
  • Information architecture
  • User flows
04

Design

Translating structure into high-fidelity screens with a robust design system.

  • Wireframes & mid-fi
  • High-fidelity UI
  • Interactive prototypes
05

Test & Iterate

Validating assumptions with real users and refining until the experience is right.

  • Usability testing
  • A/B experiments
  • Handoff & support

Hi, I'm Asad — a product designer with 3+ years of experience building apps and websites that people actually find easy to use. I focus on making complicated ideas simple, ensuring every screen I design helps your business grow and keeps your customers happy.

I've worked with everyone from new startups to established teams in finance, e-commerce, and tech. My experience spans from UI/UX design at Tech Vikings to leading design initiatives at Codioo, where I elevated user experiences and drove product vision. My work covers the whole journey — from understanding what your users want, to high-fidelity design, and delivering design-ready handoffs to development teams. I believe the best design is the one you don't have to think about: it just works. Currently, I'm open to work opportunities to help ambitious brands and products create exceptional user experiences.
